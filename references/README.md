# Review Question References

If your org runs formal review cycles (peer reviews, manager reviews, self-reflections), drop your org's actual review prompts in this folder so `/mastery` and `/peers` can pull from them when review season hits.

Suggested files:
- `self-review-questions.md` — your own self-reflection prompts
- `peer-review-questions.md` — what you have to write about peers
- `manager-review-questions.md` — what your manager will write about you

The skills look here when you mention reviews, peer reviews, or self-reflection in conversation. They use the questions to structure draft answers from your accumulated mastery entries, peer notes, and project files.

These files are gitignored by default — they're often internal to your company. Keep them local.

## Generic template

If you don't have org-specific prompts yet, here's a generic version you can adapt:

```markdown
# Self-Reflection Questions

## Open text
- Reflect on the past 6 months. Where did you have the most impact? (3 examples)
- What blocked or unlocked you?
- What's the next growth edge you want to invest in?

## Ratings (1-5)
- I set the bar through the quality of my work
- I take responsibility for outcomes, not just my own work
- I make my team and the org better
- I execute with speed
- I help others grow
```
